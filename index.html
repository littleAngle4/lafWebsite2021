Our Vision and Mission

Our Vision is the building of life where underprivileged children are empowered through quality education and care to break the cycle of poverty, positively transforming their lives and the lives of the community around them. We realize our duties and discharge them rigorously. We see a future wherein money, or the lack thereof, isn’t an impediment; a future where every child, regardless of their social class, fulfils his/her potential.

Our Projects

Support these Charity Causes by Volunteering

Saksham

We started SAKSHAM our initiative towards making education easy and safe for the children during the lockdown period. Our main motive was to provide these students with best education while siting safely at their home.

We need to collect ₹3,500 for Monthly Classes

Bharat Bhukha Na Soye

Covid-19 pandemic and lockdown made this situation even miserable. We at LAF decided to help such needy people during this period. This daily food distribution campaign started and took place in phases.

We need to collect ₹3,000

Clothes Distribution Campaign

We began our clothes distribution drive for the kids living in the slums near the Airoli D-Mart. Providing them with good education is our motto but apart from that we also take care about their basic necessities. We also planned to distribute some toys with the clothes.


Shoes Distribution Campaign

we at LAF started our shoes distribution initiative with motive to help these children get at least one pair of shoes for their daily need. We started collecting shoes and our volunteers began distribution drive for kids.
